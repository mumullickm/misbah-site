Misbah

Light in the Dark

Misbah is a calm one-touch game about carrying a small lantern of light through ornate darkness. Hold to rise, release to fall, and gather oil before the dark closes in. Free, offline, no account, no ads, no tracking.

How to play

  • One touch. Hold to lift the lantern, release to let it fall.
  • Your oil is your sight. As oil drains, the light around you contracts, so gather the glowing drops to see further.
  • There is no harsh ending. When a run ends you are met with a quiet line of reflection, and one gentle tap begins again.

Help

  • Does it work offline? Yes. Misbah plays fully in airplane mode. Nothing about your play leaves your phone.
  • Where is my progress saved? Your best score and settings are stored only on your device.
  • What are the themes? Misbah carries a Night look by default, with Ramadan and Dawn variations. On special days in the Islamic calendar the game gently takes on a fitting light, as a quiet reminder. You can turn this off in Settings.
  • Why no melodic music? Sound can be set to wind and breath only, with no instruments, for those who prefer it.
