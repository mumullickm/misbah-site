Misbah · Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Misbah collects nothing. There is nothing to collect.

Misbah is a free, fully offline game. It is built so that none of your information ever leaves your device.

Data we collect

None. Misbah does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or usage data. We have no servers and no analytics. We never see how you play.

Internet

Misbah plays fully offline and works in airplane mode. Nothing about your play is uploaded anywhere.

What is saved on your device

Your best score and your settings (such as sound, theme, and gentle mode) are saved only on your own device so the game remembers them next time. This stays on your device, is never sent anywhere, and is cleared if you remove the app or clear its data.

Accounts

Misbah has no accounts, no sign-up, and no login. We do not know who you are.

Advertising & tracking

Misbah contains no ads, no trackers, no third-party analytics, and no advertising identifiers.

Reflections

When a run ends, Misbah may show a short line of classical poetry or wisdom. These lines are part of the app and are not gathered from you or about you.

Children

Misbah is gentle and safe for all audiences. Because it collects no data, it collects no data from children either.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
